About Delicacy Catering

Your Premier Catering Partner in Kenya

Our Story

Founded in Meru, Delicacy Catering has grown to become one of Kenya's most trusted catering services. With a passion for culinary excellence and customer satisfaction, we've successfully catered events of all sizes across the country.

Our Mission

To deliver exceptional catering experiences that celebrate Kenya's diverse culinary heritage while incorporating international flavors, using locally-sourced ingredients and sustainable practices.
